Summary

*Inception* is a mind-bending sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets by entering people's dreams. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can plant an idea in someone’s subconscious — a process known as inception.

Main Actors

  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Cillian Murphy as Robert Fischer
